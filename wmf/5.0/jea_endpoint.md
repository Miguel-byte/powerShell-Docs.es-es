## <a name="connect-to-a-jea-endpoint"></a><span data-ttu-id="b6132-121">Conectarse a un punto de conexión de JEA</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b6132-121">Connect to a JEA Endpoint</span></span>
